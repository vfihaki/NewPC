About Us



Located at Toi Ohomai Institute Of Technology Windermere Campus, the Break Time Café features beautiful and wonderful dining atmosphere! Serving great drinks and food! The Break Time Café is open to public but basically for students and staff in campus.

We open Mondays to Fridays from 9am until 3pm and close in the weekends. Come along and spend your lunch breaks with us and enjoy your favourite food and drinks.

Relax on those comfy sofa or entertain yourself with friends with game of pool table or table tennis or some board games available for you.
